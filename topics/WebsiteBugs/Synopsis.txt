# The Bug and the Patterns

## Overview
*The Bug and the Patterns* is a story-driven project about a mysterious, shape-shifting creature called the bug. The story follows a character who enters a strange, endless space and discovers the bug collecting stolen designs and colors. Across four chapters, they try to stop it and contain its chaos.

## Features
- Narrative-focused, told across four chapters  
- Surreal environments, from dark voids to bright, infinite spaces  
- Suspenseful and mysterious, with a sense of discovery  
- Themes of curiosity, control, and dealing with the unknown
