This piece was the first edition of the Typitic and my first CMYK screen print. It became the foundation for the entire project. The original file was a large photograph taken at the Insectarium in Montreal. I processed this image in TouchDesigner, a visual-coding platform, where it went through a series of steps that reconfigured and reassembled it into a glitch-like composition.

I then brought the image into Photoshop, where it was further broken down into its CMYK layers. Each layer was exported as a separate file and used to create stencils for the screen-printing process.
